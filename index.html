About Me

Who am I?

I'm Scott - a student in Bournemouth University currently on his second year. My love for gaming has brought me to study Games Development. I've always been fascinated in the inner workings of a game - how certain technologies have been brought about by our genius and creativity.

I like to describe myself as someone who’s willing to do anything. I’ve tinkered with computers, created tools to edit data in spreadsheets, and I’ve even been a Rides Operator at Chessington World of Adventures! I like to dedicate myself to something, knowing that doing so will improve my drive for success and I’ll end up learning important skills I’ll take with me into the future.

My Skills

While I primarily program in and am the most confident in C++, C#, and Python, I have some experience in other languages, such as Javascript and Lua. I am familiar with using libraries such as SDL and Pygame and enjoy creating games in specialised game engines like Unreal Engine and Unity.
