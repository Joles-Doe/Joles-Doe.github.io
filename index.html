About Me

Who am I?


I'm Scott - a student in Bournemouth University currently on his second year. My love for the gaming industry has brought me to study Games Development. I've always been fascinated in the inner workings of a game - how certain technologies have been brought about in order to showcase our genius and creativity.

When I was a child, I wasn't particularly the artistic type, so I took up programming as a small hobby to pass the time. Nothing too serious, just small Scratch projects (who would torture themselves by going to C++ straight away, anyway?), but enough to pique my interest. From highschool, I moved onto python, creating small programs in order to get to grips with text-based languages. But I didn't want to just program - I wanted to make games. My search in that aspect led me to Pygame, where I showcased the majority of my skills into making small games. From college, I kept using pygame, progressively making more and more complex games whilst learning other languages on the side. That brings me to now - a hopeful academic who hopes to make a difference in the gaming world when he leaves university.
